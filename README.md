# Sales-Performance-Dashboard-Analysis
A complete end-to-end data analytics project focused on analyzing sales performance, identifying revenue drivers, and generating actionable business insights using Power BI + SQL.

🧭 Project Overview

This project analyzes sales data across products, brands, and categories to uncover:

Revenue & profit trends
High-performing products
Category-wise performance
Monthly growth patterns
Profitability insights

👉 Goal: Enable data-driven business decisions to improve revenue and optimize profitability.

🔍 Key Insights

1. Revenue Drivers
Scarlett 2i2 is the top-performing product (~844K revenue)
Contributes ~28.5% of total revenue
Strong demand makes it a key growth driver
2. Category Performance
Audio Interface leads (~0.84M revenue)
Condenser & Recording categories follow closely
Opportunity to improve lower-performing categories
3. Brand Analysis
Focusrite dominates revenue contribution
Mid-tier: Rode, PreSonus
Low performers: SteelSeries
4. Monthly Trends
Revenue shows a fluctuating but upward trend
Peaks observed mid-year and year-end
Profit closely follows the revenue trend
5. Profitability
Overall margin: 31.64%
Some products exceed 50% margin
Indicates a strong pricing strategy, but room for cost optimization
